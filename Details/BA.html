BlessedAid is a centralized platform connecting individuals with NGOs, focusing on transparency, trust, and accessibility in social causes. It enables secure donations, crowdfunding participation, event ticketing, and drive registrations — all in one place.


The platform ensures transparency in donation tracking and campaign progress, fostering trust and encouraging active engagement. NGOs can manage campaigns, track donations, and share real-time updates, while users can easily participate in and support various initiatives.



Key Features:

  • 💳 Secure Donations: Integrated payment gateway for quick and safe transactions.
  • 📅 Event & Drive Management: NGOs can create, manage, and track events and drives.
  • 🔍 Searchable NGO Directory: Find NGOs based on causes, locations, or campaigns.
  • 📢 Real-Time Updates: Instant notifications for campaign progress and events.
  • 📊 Donation Tracking Dashboards: Transparent records for donors and NGOs.
